Advancement Requirements List

Venturing Rank
Rank Focus : Joining

The first step is to learn about Venturing, what the crew does, what to expect from your experiences with the crew, and making a commitment to the principles of Venturing.

Discovery Rank
Rank Focus : Participation

The adventure of participating begins with discovering his or her interests and talents and develop new skills and competencies, their eyes are opened, the world expands for them.

Pathfinder Rank
Rank Focus : Leadership

At this level, you will demonstrate your leadership skills by organizing and guiding the crew on adventures. You will continue to deliver service to others, including your crew.

Summit Rank
Rank Focus : Mentoring

The Summit Rank is achieved by serving the crew as a leader and a mentor to others, while strengthening your community by designing and leading a service project.
